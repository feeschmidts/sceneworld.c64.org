The Crew

The SceneWorld Crew is a group of 15 geeks with interest in C64 and gaming. Quarterly the group works together on new issues interviewing old and new faces of the Commodore C64 world. We are a group of internationals and communicate via facebook. We're from Germany, States, UK, Brazil ....
