The Crew

The SceneWorld Crew is a group of 15 geeks with interest in C64 and gaming. Quarterly the group works together on new issues interviewing old and new faces of the Commodore C64 world. We are a group of internationals and communicate via facebook. We're from Germany, States, UK, Brazil ....

Nafcom - Net Trader, Diskmag Editor, Organizer | feeschmidts - Design, Website | Alwyz - Net Trader / Editor | Black - Coder | Derision - Graphician, Editor | Drake - Graphician, Editor | Jon - Editor | JSL - Graphician, Editor | Merman - Coder, Organizerm Musician, Editor, Swapper | Nebulous - Webmaster | Richard - Diskmag Editor, Musician | Six - Coder, NTSC-Fixer, Tester, Diskmag Editor | The Overkiller - Swapper, Public Relations Manager, Net Trader, Diskmag Editor | TMC - Diskmag Editor | V-12 - Diskmag Editor | Macbeth - Coder, NTSC-Fixer, PAL-Fixer
